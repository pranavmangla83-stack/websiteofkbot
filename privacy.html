Custom AI Chatbot

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2026

Custom AI Chatbot helps businesses answer website visitor questions using documents uploaded by the business owner.

Information we collect

We may collect account details, uploaded business documents, chatbot messages, usage records, payment status, and visitor contact details submitted through the chatbot fallback form.

How we use information

We use this information to run the chatbot, process documents, provide support, maintain subscriptions, prevent abuse, and help businesses respond to visitor leads.

Payments

Payments are processed through Razorpay. We do not store card, UPI, or banking details on our servers.

Data sharing

We use service providers such as hosting, database, authentication, AI processing, analytics, and payment tools. We do not sell customer data.

Business documents

Uploaded documents are used to create chatbot answers for the business account that uploaded them. Business owners should not upload documents they are not allowed to use.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the business owner or support contact provided by Custom AI Chatbot.
