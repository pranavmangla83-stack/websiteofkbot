FAQ

Common questions

Do I need coding?

You do not need coding to upload documents or manage the chatbot. To install it, paste one script into your website. Your web developer can do this quickly if needed.

Can I upload scanned PDFs?

Yes. The system includes OCR processing for scanned PDFs, though clear text PDFs usually give better results.

Can I add it to any website?

Yes, if your website allows adding a script. Set your allowed website URL in the dashboard, then paste the chatbot script into the site.

What if the chatbot does not know the answer?

It should avoid guessing. It can show a fallback response, ask for contact details, or tell the visitor to contact support.

Is payment monthly?

Yes. The Basic plan is ₹350 per month as a recurring subscription.
