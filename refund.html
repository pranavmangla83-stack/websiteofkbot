Refund Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2026

The Basic plan is a monthly subscription for small business website chatbot usage.

Monthly billing

Subscription payments are charged monthly through Razorpay. You can stop future billing by cancelling the subscription.

Refund requests

If you were charged by mistake or the service could not be activated, contact support within 7 days of payment with your account email and payment details.

Non-refundable cases

Refunds may not be available after active usage of the chatbot, document processing, or completed monthly service delivery, except where required by law.

Processing

Approved refunds are processed through the original Razorpay payment method and may take several business days depending on the bank or payment provider.
